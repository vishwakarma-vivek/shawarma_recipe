Chicken Shawarma

This is a delicious recipe for chicken shawarma. It's packed with flavor and easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Shawarma spice mix
  • Garlic cloves
  • Plain yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Vegetables (tomatoes, cucumbers, onions)
  • Pita bread
  • Tahini sauce

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken breast with shawarma spice mix, garlic, yogurt, and lemon juice for at least 1 hour.
  2. Grill the chicken until cooked through.
  3. Slice the chicken and serve in warm pita bread with vegetables and tahini sauce.
