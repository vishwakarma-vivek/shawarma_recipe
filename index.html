Chicken Shawarma

This is a delicious recipe for chicken shawarma. It's packed with flavor and easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Shawarma spice mix
  • Garlic cloves
  • Plain yogurt
  • Lemon juice
  • Vegetables (tomatoes, cucumbers, onions)
  • Pita bread
  • Tahini sauce

Instructions:

  1. Marinate the chicken breast with shawarma spice mix, garlic, yogurt, and lemon juice for at least 1 hour.
  2. Grill the chicken until cooked through.
  3. Slice the chicken and serve in warm pita bread with vegetables and tahini sauce.

Vegetarian Shawarma

Enjoy this delicious vegetarian shawarma with a medley of flavorful vegetables.

Ingredients:

  • Falafel
  • Hummus
  • Baba ganoush
  • Tabbouleh
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Onions
  • Pita bread

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the falafel according to the package instructions.
  2. Warm the pita bread and spread a layer of hummus and baba ganoush.
  3. Stuff the pita bread with falafel, tabbouleh, tomatoes, cucumbers, and onions.
